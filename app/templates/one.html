Measuring Snowpack

Snow Type

Snow water equivalency is calculated by knowing snowpack's density and depth. Different types of snow have different densities. Using the following equation SWE = Density * Depth, snow water equivalency can be approximated. April 1st is generally considered the ideal day to predict water flowing downstream.
