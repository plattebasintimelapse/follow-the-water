Situated just below the Sweetwater and the North Platte rivers' confluence, Pathfinder Dam's 214ft of grantite was quarried and constructed on-site from 1905-1909.

At it's time, Pathfinder Dam was a tremendous undertaking. Four people died during its construction.

The top spans the girth of Fremont Canyon, offering dramatic views down steep sides to the river below.
